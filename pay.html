The CodeCartel focuses projects designed to help contributors:

  • Open source projects you can use to help you achieve goals
  • Simplistic software systems that do not require a lot code
  • Projects that pay you a share of revenue with MoneyService
Projects must have a customer lifetime value of $500 or more.
The financial model should use a series of one-time payments.
Payments go from the end client directly to each contributor.
Transactions are anonymized and published online in realtime.
